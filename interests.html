What are your Students' Interests? | Emergent Curriculum

Another jumping off point for creating a literature-based unit could be your children's interest? For example, are they particuluarly excited about ocean life? dinosaurs? or fairytales? Choosing a book with a topic, main characters, or world that you know will catch your students interests will go a long way toward engaging them in your curriculum.

What is Emergent Curriculum?

The University of Toronto explains that emergent curriculum is

based on the premise that children are most successful at learning when curriculum experiences account for their interests, strengths, needs, and lived realities. Educators committed to this philosophy use observations of children throughout their day as a tool for constructing curriculum content.
