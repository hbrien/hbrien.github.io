Welcome Teachers

This website will give you a step-by-step guide on how to create a literature-based learning unit for preschoolers.

Whether you're looking for more ways to add literacy into your classroom schedule, need support in choosing quality pieces of literature that will keep children engaged, or wanting a more emergent approach that draws from your students' interests, this is the place for you. Below you will find the links to each of these topics. Choose the one that interests you the most and start there.

This website demonstrates effective design for instruction by offering a clean, consice, and clear interface for users to interact with. The website also reduces cognitive load by separating each topic onto its own page.
